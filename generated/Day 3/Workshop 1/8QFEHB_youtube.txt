Demos and presentations covering a wide range of applications of zero knowledge proofs, from light clients to machine learning to spam-resistant messaging and anonymous social media. Focusing more on the application layer than deep technical details, this session will provide an overview of the applied ZK landscape for people at any level of technical knowledge.
